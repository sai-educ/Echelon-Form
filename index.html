What is Rank?
The rank is the number of linearly independent rows. We find it by reducing to echelon form and counting non-zero rows.

Integer Operations:

  • Swap: Ri ‚Üî Rj
  • Add: Ri ‚Üê Ri + k¬∑Rj
Key: Rank tells us the dimension of column space and system solvability!
